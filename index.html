OCEAN BREATH

A WEEKEND YOGA RETREAT WITH ALVA & DANI

Friday 1st March 2019, from 5pm - Monday 4th March, 1pm 2019
Northcourt House, Isle of Wight

We would love to invite you to join us for our second retreat together - join us for Ocean Breath, a weekend yoga retreat on the peaceful Isle of Wight

Ocean Breath Yoga Retreat - come, explore, dive deep, enjoy and come away fully revived

Let us take you on a little adventure. Join us for an immersive and uplifting experience as we dive into the world of yoga, meditation and into ourselves.
As we cross over the sea and we arrive on the beautiful Isle of Wight we can fully dedicate our time to ourselves. We will have plenty of time for asana practice and pranayama and as we have 3 nights away we have time to explore a little more even.
Gong baths, long countryside walks, home cooked vegetarian/vegan food and the option to explore different types of meditation.
The weekend will nourish us from the inside out - expect to relax, learn something new, enjoy, connect, experience & flourish.

Hosted by Alva (Alva Yoga) and Dani (Healthy Whole Me)

Talk to us at info@alvayoga.com or call +44 (0) 7947444194

Your retreat location - home for the weekend

We invite you to share the beautiful historic Jacobean manor house on the Isle of Wight with us. Situated on the south-west side of the Isle of Wight, Northcourt Manor was built in 1615 on the site of a monastic building.
The 15 acre, glorious garden enjoys a very special microclimate, nestled below the Downs between the chalk and greensand ridges. All of which is ours to explore for the weekend.

Northcourt House

There are many ways of getting to Northcourt House. You can either take the train to Portsmouth and then the ferry (for which we will get discounted tickets).
On the other side we will be happy to collect you by car. We will also connect us all in a WhatsApp group and we can arrange car-shares.
Once you arrive at Northcourt House you will feel immediately at home. Our retreat chef Sophie will be cooking in the kitchen which also serves as our dining room, so the house will be smelling amazing all day long. There’s no need to bring anything, this retreat is all-inclusive and we will make sure to have teas and food on tap for you 24/7.
The best bit about our weekend home is the stunning yoga shala in which we will spend many fantastic hours.
Northcourt House offers us twin bedrooms and we will be able to talk to you about sleeping arrangements so that you are all comfortable in your rooms.

This is YOUR retreat - do as much or as little as you wish

We are very excited to create this weekend retreat for you and it is our utmost desire to make you feel as comfortable as possible. Once you’ve arrived you don’t need to worry about anything at all.
We will put together a weekend schedule for you which will include your yoga asana practices, gong baths, mediation workshops, mindfulness sessions and we will also plan in a trip or two to explore the beautiful Isle of Wight.
We really want to spoil you, from the food that will be cooked for you to the sessions we plan, but we also want you to be a able to relax, sleep as much as you want and spend time on your own if you wish.
This is YOUR time away from home, we want you to do whatever you like!

What to bring

Please bring your yoga mat if you have one - it’s lovely taking it on a journey! A couple of sets of yoga clothes are great and apart from that - it is ALL casual! Bring an outdoor jacket, waterproofs and walking boots or wellies for our country walks. Last but not least, a refillable water bottle comes in handy too!
